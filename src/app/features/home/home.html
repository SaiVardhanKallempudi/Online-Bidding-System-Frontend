Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a bidder?

After creating an account, go to "Become a Bidder" and submit your application. Admin will review within 24-48 hours.

Is there a registration fee?

No, creating an account and applying as a bidder is completely free. You only pay when you win an auction.

How does live bidding work?

Once an auction starts, place bids in real-time. The highest bidder when the timer ends wins the stall. You'll receive instant notifications if outbid.

What happens after I win?

You'll receive a confirmation email with payment instructions and stall allocation details.
